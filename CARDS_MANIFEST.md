## Design Specifications

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Dark Green #0F3D1F
- **Accent**: Gold #D4A017
- **Text**: Cream #F8F5F0
- **Supporting**: Gray scale for contrast

### Dimensions
- Canvas: Full-screen (optimized for 1920x1080)
- Safe area: Full viewport with padding
- Scalable to mobile/tablet

### Typography
- Family: System fonts (Segoe UI, San Francisco, Helvetica Neue)
- Headings: Bold, large (48-60px)
- Body: Regular, readable (16-24px)
- High contrast for accessibility

### Animations
- Fade-in on card load: 600ms
- Smooth button hovers
- No distracting transitions
- Clean, professional feel

## Navigation System

### Features
- **10 total slides** (indexed 0-9)
- **Arrow key support**: Left/Right navigation
- **Button controls**: Previous/Next buttons
- **Quick jump**: 10 indicator dots at top
- **Slide counter**: Shows current position (e.g., "5 / 10")
- **Keyboard hint**: Bottom-right display

### Controls Location
- **Top**: Slide indicator dots (clickable)
- **Bottom**: Previous/Next buttons + Counter
- **Right**: Keyboard hint text
- **Top-left**: Current slide title
